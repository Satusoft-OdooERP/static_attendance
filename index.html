Payroll With
Attendance

Overview

Get Your Employees Attendance Number Directly Into Payslip Calculation (Attendance)

Features

  • Get Directly Employee Attendance
  • Get Attendance Number Of Days and Hours product.

How To

1. Import Your Employees Attendance or Use Odoo Attendance Directly .

Before use this module, you need to fill your employee attendance first , Whether is imported from fingerprint machine or using Odoo Attendance System .

2. Get Your Employees Attendance Lines.

After the first step , Generate a new payroll for the employee , and if you generate with the range of date then you get instantly the number of the employee attendance within that range of date .
